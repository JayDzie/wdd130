White Water Rafting Adventures

Prepared by: Joel Dadzie

Section: WDD130

Purpose

The White Water Rafting Adventures website aims to provide thrilling white water rafting experiences. Our goal is to attract adventure enthusiasts and outdoor lovers looking for an exciting and memorable river adventure.

Audience

Our target audience includes individuals and groups who:

  • Seek adrenaline-pumping outdoor activities.
  • Enjoy water sports and adventure tourism.
  • Are looking for a unique and challenging experience.

They are likely to access our site on various devices, including desktop computers, laptops, and mobile devices.
